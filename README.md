# Academic Website

https://naataaniitsosie.github.io/academic-website/

A professional academic website template with an industry experience section, perfect for academics with a background in industry.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Industry Experience Section**: Dedicated area to showcase your industry background
- **Complete Sections**:
  - Home/Hero section
  - About me
  - Education history
  - Industry experience (with job titles, companies, and achievements)
  - Research interests
  - Publications
  - Contact information
  - Social media links

## Getting Started

1. **Edit the HTML file (`index.html`)**:
   - Replace placeholder text with your actual information
   - Update the navigation menu if needed
   - Add your profile picture URL in the About section

2. **Customize the CSS file (`styles.css`)**:
   - Change color scheme (currently uses blue: `#1a4d7a`, `#2a6a9a`)
   - Adjust fonts and spacing as needed
   - Modify the responsive breakpoints

3. **Key Placeholders to Replace**:
   - `[Your Field]` - Your academic discipline
   - `[University Name]` - Your institutions
   - `[Job Title]` - Your industry positions
   - `[Company Name]` - Your employers
   - `your.email@university.edu` - Your contact email
   - `[Building Name], Room [Number]` - Your office location
   - `[Your Phone Number]` - Your contact number
   - Profile picture URL (LinkedIn, GitHub URLs, social media)

## Color Scheme

The default color scheme uses:
- **Primary Blue**: `#1a4d7a`
- **Secondary Blue**: `#2a6a9a`
- **Light Blue**: `#a0d8ff`
- **Light Background**: `#f0f7ff`

Feel free to customize these colors in `styles.css` to match your preferences.

## Sections Breakdown

### Home
A welcoming hero section with a gradient background that introduces your profile.

### About
Personal introduction with an optional profile picture and biography.

### Education
Chronological list of your academic qualifications (Ph.D., Master's, Bachelor's, etc.).

### Industry Experience ⭐
Showcase your professional background with:
- Job titles
- Company names
- Duration of employment
- Key responsibilities and achievements
- Styled cards with hover effects

### Research
Your main research interests and areas of focus, highlighting how your industry experience informs your research.

### Publications
A list of your academic publications with descriptions and links.

### Contact
Contact information and social media links (LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, etc.).

## Customization Tips

1. **Change Colors**: Update the hex color codes in `styles.css`
2. **Add More Positions**: Copy the `.experience-item` div in the Industry Experience section for additional roles
3. **Modify Layout**: Use CSS Grid and Flexbox properties in `styles.css` to adjust layouts
4. **Add Images**: Replace the placeholder image URL with your actual profile picture
5. **Update Links**: Add real links to your publications, social media, and contact methods

## Responsive Design

The website is fully responsive with breakpoints at:
- **768px** (tablets)
- **480px** (mobile phones)

All sections adapt gracefully to smaller screens.

## Browser Compatibility

Compatible with all modern browsers:
- Chrome/Chromium
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

## Deploying Your Website

You can host this website on:
- **GitHub Pages** (free, simple)
